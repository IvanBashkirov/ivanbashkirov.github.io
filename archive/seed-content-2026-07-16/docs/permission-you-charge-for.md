Every discovery call, part of me waits for someone to say the idea is allowed. Nobody ever will. A pre-commitment is just permission you charge for.

I caught the waiting on call Nº 4, somewhere between the demo I did not have and the roadmap I should not have mentioned. What I wanted from the coach was a blessing. What I eventually asked for was a deposit. The difference is that a blessing costs the giver nothing and expires on contact with their actual week, while a deposit is the same sentence with consequences attached. So the gate stays where it is: ChallengeClub gets built when three people have said yes in the only dialect I still trust.
