Research is procrastination with a clean conscience. This log exists because I know that about myself, and because a public number is harder to argue with than a private plan.

Nobody has ever looked at a person deep in competitor teardowns and accused them of hiding, which is exactly what makes the teardowns dangerous. I can convert a market report into a fortnight without anyone, including me, noticing the exchange rate. The log does not accept reading as an entry type. It accepts shipped, wrote, filed, learned — verbs with residue. When the counter stalls, I do not need to ask what I have been doing. I have been researching.
